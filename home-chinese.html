Frequently Asked Questions

Will there be a presale?

There will be two phases of presale:
‍
Phase 1 - Whitelist, which is now a PRIVATE SALE
Phase 2 - DxSale public presale on June 18th following completion of the PRIVATE SALE

We are still accepting applications for our Whitelist through our Sweepwidget link.
‍
The Private Sale raise will still follow the soft cap/hard cap guidlines laid out in our Whitepaper.  The original Unicrypt fee allocation will be added to our Liquidity Pool.The Whitelist is now a PRIVATE SALE, your BUSD contribution will result in a distribution of tokens BEFORE our DxSale launch.
‍
There are at the time of writing still rouglhy 170 / 500 slots available on the Whitelist.Whitelist Minumum / Maximum contribution amounts have been changed from 50 BUSD minumum / 500 BUSD maximum.

Acceptance onto the Whitelist with a successful contribution of BUSD will result in the distribution of tokens to your wallet before our DxSale Phase 2 on June 18th.
‍
Private sale token price will be 20% less than DxSale and PancakeSwap listing price.DxSale and Pancakeswap listing prices will be the same.Please do not send more than the 500 BUSD maximum contribution.  IF YOU SEND MORE THAN THE LIMIT, IT WILL BE REFUNDED MINUS THE GAS IT COSTS TO REFUND THE AMOUNT. We are using a multi-sig wallet to custody the private sale funds and gas is expensive!  Your over-contribution will be returned to you but with gas fees deducted.  Please only send no more than 500 BUSD - THIS IS THE MAXIMUM.

We have discussed these ratios, amounts, and limits at great length as a team to ensure that this launch is as fair to everyone as possible.  We want to incentivize and reward our community and early adopters with a better price than public listing but also gaurd against abuse and the possibility of pump and dump activity on new buyers. 
‍
We believe that these ratios provide the best balance possible and we hope you agree.  Every decision we make is with the long term health, longevity, and safety of our investors in mind. We intend on nothing less than Sonar being a Blue Chip project in the DeFi space.

The tokenomics in our Whitepaper will be updated to reflect these changes after completion of the private sale but the FUNDAMENTALS will not change whatsoever.  Presale allocation ratios will be adjusted to reflect how we now intend to launch.  Once we complete the private sale we will have enough information about the total contributions to update the Whitepaper.

DxSale does not support BUSD, and will accept presale in BNB.  However, we will still be launching our Liquidity pool with a BUSD pair.

Where will Sonar ($PING) be listed?

After completion of the presale we will be listed on PancakeSwap.

When CoinGecko / CoinMarketCap?

The moment we meet the holder and market cap requirements for listing we will be submitting applications.

合约的所有权会被放弃吗？

我们不会放弃所有权。 繁琐的合约迁移会限制了我们实施必要更改和升级的能力。 很多项目在从 PCS v1 迁移到 v2 后就从未恢复了。 这只是我们预计随着空间不断变化而需要适应的许多事情中的一个例子。

如果你不放弃所有权，作为投资者的我，怎么能确定你不跑路？

放弃所有权已成为 鱼龙混杂的BSC链上的常态，但实际上只是一种噱头，只会提供安全的假象。 我们为潜在投资者提供了多层保护。 LP 池会被锁定。
我们的合约代码已经过安全漏洞审计。 推出后，我们将寻求 CertiK 更高级别的审核。 核心军官团队也完全KYC实名。 营销和开发钱包只能通过多重签名技术访问，必须由官员团队的所有成员签名才能释放资金。
我们和其他人一样，对欺诈和诈骗的泛滥感到厌烦和厌倦。 我们的使命、我们的身份和我们唯一的目标是尽我们所能改变这个令人讨厌的现实。 我们知道在这个领域很难获得信任，因此我们从会从点滴做起，不着急慢慢来。
